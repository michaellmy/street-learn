StreetLearn: An Opportunity for Everyone.

An eLearning platform to help homeless and street children to obtain skillsets, using our custom chatbot to fit your preferences.

About StreetLearn

StreetLearn is an educational platform website that can be accessed by street youth and the homeless to obtain skills education. You can access lessons and training made available from the Open Educational Resources (OER) through a chatbot that recommmends suitable material for you using Artificial Intelligence, based on your preferences. These teaching material was made available through the X5GON API. It is a platform intended to help the community by providing them with suitable skills education that may be applied in the future.

Why StreetLearn?

  • Offers a large variety of resources, in the form of videos, textbooks and so on.
  • Recommends learning material based on your preferences.

Introducing StreetBot!

StreetBot is our personalized chatbot that recommends the best skills training for you. StreetBot does this using various key algorithms by reviewing any preferences or previous backgrounds that you have through Artificial Intelligence. It can redirect you to suitable training courses that are available from the Open Education Resources (OER) through the X5GON API.


Powered by X5GON

X5GON (https://platform.x5gon.org) is an international project supported by UCL Computer Science to share Open Education Resources (OERs).

Open Education Resources (OER)

OER offers resources such videos, lectures, teaching materials, forms and sample data for education. These resources are obtained through an X5GON API.
