LearnStreet: An Opportunity for Everyone.

An eLearning platform to help homeless and street children to obtain skillsets, using our custom chatbot to fit your preferences.

About StreetLearn

StreetLearn is an educational platform website that can be accessed by street youth and the homeless to obtain skills and training. You can access a chatbot that recommends you lessons and training made available form the Open Educational Resources through a chatbot that uses Artificial Intelligence, based on their preferences. It is a platform intended to help the community by providing them with suitable skills and traning that may be applied in the future.

Why StreetLearn?

  • Offers a large variety of resources.
  • Recommends these resources based on your preferences.
  • Is completely free to use!

Introducing StreetBot!

StreetBot is our personalized chatbot that recommends the best training for you. StreetBot does this using various key algorithms by reviewing any preferences or previous backgrounds that you have, through Artificial Intelligence. It can redirect you to suitable training courses that are available from the Open Education Resources (OER).

Powered by X5GON

X5GON (https://platform.x5gon.org) is an international project supported by UCL Computer Science to share Open Education Resources (OERs).

Open Education Resources (OER)

OER offers resources such videos, lectures, teaching materials, forms and sample data for education. Many institutes around the world are contributing their education resources as well.
